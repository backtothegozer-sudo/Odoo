The launch of ChatGPT for Excel turns an already ubiquitous tool into a mass AI interface. For companies, the opportunity is significant, but sovereignty depends on precise decisions about data, access rights, and action traceability.

1. Pourquoi cette news compte for l'sovereign AI

Excel remains one of the main entry points for financial, commercial, and operational data. Adding an AI assistant in this context immediately increases productivity, but also expands risk exposure: unintentional extraction of sensitive data, large-scale error propagation, and loss of visibility over applied transformations.

2. Concrete impact on IT and compliance teams

The objective is not to block usage, but to frame it. A sovereign strategy requires classifying allowed file types, segmenting permissions by role, and logging critical operations. Without these controls, the tool becomes fast but difficult to audit.

3. Decisions to make in the next 30 days

Three measures provide immediate effect: define a spreadsheet data usage policy by sensitivity level, deploy a controlled pilot on a limited business perimeter, and establish a monthly review mechanism for high-risk prompts/results. This approach preserves execution speed while protecting governance.
