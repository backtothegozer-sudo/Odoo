The extension of EuroHPC’s mandate sends a strong signal: European sovereign AI is no longer a theoretical scenario, but an institutionally supported infrastructure trajectory.

1. Why this is strategic for companies

IT leadership teams need visibility to commit serious investments. EuroHPC provides a clearer framework to plan compliant, localized architectures, reducing dependency on non-European options for the most sensitive workloads.

2. Ce que cela change dans the feuille de route

Instead of reasoning only in the short term, companies can structure a two-speed trajectory: stabilize immediate use cases with strong governance, then progressively migrate critical workloads to robust European hosting targets.

3. Recommended governance

Shared governance across CIO, security, and business teams is essential. Success depends on coherence between architecture, compliance, cost, and business value—not on a single isolated technical decision.
