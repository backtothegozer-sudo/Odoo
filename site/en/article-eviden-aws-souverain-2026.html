Eviden x AWS European Sovereign Cloud: a milestone for sovereign AI

Article created on 10 March 2026 · Event analyzed: 10 March 2026 · Source: Atos / Eviden (GlobeNewswire)

Share inLinkedIn XX fFacebook

Eviden joining as a launch partner of AWS European Sovereign Cloud adds a very concrete building block to sovereign AI: external control of encryption keys, with European anchoring, for the most sensitive workloads.

1. Pourquoi cette news compte for l'sovereign AI

Sovereignty is not limited to data localization. It also depends on who controls keys, access, and audit evidence. Eviden’s positioning highlights a model where the master key can remain outside the cloud, reducing exposure for environments under strong regulatory constraints.

2. Impact direct for the DSI and RSSI

For critical AI workloads (healthcare, finance, public sector, defense), this type of architecture increases assurance levels without blocking innovation. Teams can keep high compliance requirements while remaining on operational platforms at scale.

3. Decisions to make in the next 30 days

Three actions are priorities: map datasets that must remain under external key control, define an encryption policy by risk class, and launch a technical pilot with performance, auditability, and reversibility criteria.
