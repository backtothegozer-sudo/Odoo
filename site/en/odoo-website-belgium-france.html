An Odoo site should not try to do everything at once.   • Create pages that target a concrete need instead of a vague promise.
  • Differentiate Belgium and France messaging when proof points or references are not the same.

3. Connect forms to a usable CRM process

A form only matters if incoming requests can be qualified and handled without friction.
