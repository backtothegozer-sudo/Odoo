Frequently asked questions.

Have a specific context? We can schedule a short discovery session and provide a clear next-step plan.

Can Odoo work if we already use multiple systems?

Yes. We map your existing stack and define what should be replaced, kept or integrated to avoid duplicate data and fragmented workflows.

Can you recover an Odoo project that stalled?

Yes. We run a functional and technical audit, identify blockers and propose a phased recovery plan with measurable milestones.

Do you build industry-specific custom modules?

Yes. When standard modules are not enough, we build and maintain targeted extensions with clean, documented code.

What happens after go-live?

We provide post-launch support, quick fixes, prioritized enhancements and ongoing coaching to improve operational performance.
