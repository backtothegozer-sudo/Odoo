The first 90 days after go-live: support and improvement

FR EN

An Odoo project does not end at go-live. The first 90 days are decisive for long-term trust and business value realization. Without structured run governance, recurring pain points quickly erode adoption and performance.

Post go-live should be managed as an execution program: incident triage, fit-for-purpose SLAs, enhancement prioritization, and regular benefit reviews. This structure converts user feedback into concrete operational gains.

Recommended run governance

  • Close-support cell on business-critical processes.
  • Weekly review of incidents and corrective actions.
  • Monthly prioritization of enhancement backlog.
  • Tracking of adoption and execution KPIs.

Prioritization quality depends on request qualification quality. Each item should be categorized: blocking incident, minor correction, optimization, or strategic evolution. This prevents urgent issues from being mixed with comfort requests.

For client and prospect communication, highlight continuity of ownership: deployment is a milestone, sustained performance is the objective. This posture distinguishes implementation-only delivery from true long-term partnership.

Organizations that stabilize quickly after go-live keep short decision loops, clear responsibilities, and measurable outcomes.
